Site Name

WanderWell Travel – This name represents a travel resource website focused on helping solo and group travelers plan personalized adventures with wellness and sustainability in mind.

Optional domain availability: wanderwelltravel.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the WanderWell Travel site is to provide curated travel guides, wellness-centered trip ideas, eco-friendly accommodation options, and tools to plan personalized trips. It also features a community section for members to share travel experiences and tips.

Scenarios

  • What are the top sustainable resorts in Costa Rica?
  • How do I build a personalized wellness-focused itinerary for Southeast Asia?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color – #168e36 (moonstone): Used for headings and buttons to convey a sense of wellness and nature.
  • Accent Color – #a6c8d7 (cadet-gray): Used for highlights, icons, and call-to-action backgrounds.

Typography

  • Heading Font – "Roboto", sans-serif: Elegant serif font used for headings to evoke a premium feel.
  • Body Font – Roboto: Clean sans-serif font used for all paragraph and body content for easy readability.
